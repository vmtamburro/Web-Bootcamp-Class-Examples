How to Succeed

  1. Define Your Goals

    2. What is it that YOU want to get out of this bootcamp? Are you preparing for an interview, trying to get some more coding practice, or maybe trying a new career path? If you define your goals early you are much more likely to achieve them.

  2. Learn by doing

    3. The only way to learn how to code is to do it! Try to implement the topics you are introduced to on your own. Play around with what you think might look good or try to think of an elegant way to solve a problem. The more you tinker with these concepts, the faster you will get it!

  3. Review Material

    4. Computer Science topics, especially very abstract ones, take a lot of time to digest and internalize. Take good notes and review the material daily. When you truly understand a concept, it will be an integrated part of your programming!

  4. Don't Procrastinate

    5. Chip away at homework/projects a little bit every day. If you run into roadblocks, you will find that the solution may come to you if you give it enough time! Give yourself time to think about complex solutions and ask questions.

  5. Don't Give Up!

    6. Don't shy away from topics that you do not yet understand. It will all come with time and practice! Learning programming is all about overcoming obstacles and keeping those solutions in your pocket for the next time you encounter that problem!
